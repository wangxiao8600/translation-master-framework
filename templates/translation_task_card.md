Do not place private source paths, raw logs, full prompt chains, unpublished manuscript fragments, or sensitive production notes in this card.

## 11. Acceptance Checks

The translation is ready for the next stage when:

- the core structure is preserved
- key terms stay within approved boundaries
- the target language reads naturally to native readers
- narrative rhythm remains coherent
- emotional force is not flattened
- cultural references are handled deliberately
- AI-generated stiffness has been reduced through review
- unresolved risks are documented
